Why you might have Insomnia

By Adi

Insomnia is a common sleep disorder that affects millions of people worldwide. It's characterized by dissatifcation in falling asleep, staying asleep, or both. Insomnia can be acute (short-term) or chronic (long-term), and it can have a variety of causes.

source

Prevalence

According to Levenson, Kay, & Buysse (2015), prevalence rates of insomnia disorder is set between at 5% to 15% of people worldwide.
